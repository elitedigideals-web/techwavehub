## How to Choose the Right Laptop Under $500



![Choosing a Laptop](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1460925895917-afdab827c52f?w=800)



Before buying a budget laptop, consider these key factors:

**RAM:** Always go for at least 8GB. 16GB is better if you plan to multitask or use multiple browser tabs.

**Storage:** An SSD is a must. Even a 256GB SSD will make your laptop feel significantly faster than an older HDD model.

**Display:** Look for Full HD (1920x1080) resolution minimum. An IPS or OLED panel gives better colors and viewing angles.

**Battery Life:** Aim for at least 7 hours of real-world battery life if you plan to use it on the go.

**Processor:** AMD Ryzen 5 or Intel Core i5 are the sweet spots for performance at this price range.
